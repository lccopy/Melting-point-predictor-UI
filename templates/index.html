Melting Point Estimation App

Enter your SMILES structure and get the estimated melting point with accuracy.

SMILES (Simplified Molecular Input Line Entry System) is a notation that allows a user to represent a chemical structure in a way that can be used by the computer. It is a text string that encodes a molecular structure.

The melting point of a substance is crucial for identification and characterization of a compound. It is an essential physical property used in many fields of science and industry.

Our estimation tool uses a combination of machine learning algorithms trained on a large dataset of known chemical compounds and their melting points. The tool considers various molecular descriptors and properties to predict melting points.

The data used for training our prediction models is sourced from reputable chemical databases and scientific literature. It encompasses a wide range of organic and inorganic compounds.
